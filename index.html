A premiere boarding facility located in Renton, Washington within minutes of Seattle. Homestead Horse Boarding is a full care facility offering large covered and outdoor arenas, comfortable matted stalls, individual turnout, trails, and secure trailer parking. 25 years of experience at this location has strengthened our resolve to make the equine experience as pleasant as possible. Homestead management understands that horses have different needs so we believe in treating each horse individually. Horses are not warehoused at homestead.
